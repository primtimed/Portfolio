Drop your background video here as:

    background.mp4

It will automatically be used as the fixed, full-page background video
(referenced at `/videos/background.mp4`). Until a file is placed here,
the site just shows the dark gradient background with no video.

Tips:

- Keep it short and loopable (a few seconds to ~30s works well).
- Keep the file size small (a few MB) since it autoplays on page load.
- No audio track needed — it always plays muted.
